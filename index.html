Here at The Book Club, our strength is our diversity. We encourage people of all ages and from all background to join. Sharing our own, varied experiences will open up a wealth of potential meaning in any of our chosen books.

So please, come and share your individuality with us!

We have a range of fantastic venues for our meetings. These are often public spaces with plentiful parking!

Inside, we have arranged for comfortable tables and chairs. These may be arranged in a horse-shoe or in group, whichever suits you best! Also, there will be no need to bring refreshments - they're all provided.

Why not pop in?

The unknown can be scary sometimes. So why not come along to one of our meetings, ask questions and get to know people?
